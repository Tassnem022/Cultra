Our mission

Why we built this

Most of what people know about other cultures comes from Wikipedia, travel blogs written by outsiders, or stereotypes from media. None of that is the real thing.

Cultura gives you three things no encyclopedia can — a real map you can navigate, the human stories behind iconic food, and voices directly from the people who live that culture every day.

We built this for Codex Egypt 2026 under the theme "World Without Borders" — because we believe the biggest borders are not physical. They are in our minds.
